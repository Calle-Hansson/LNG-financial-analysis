Project overview

This project set out to explore if there could be a correlation between LNG Exports from the largest LNG terminal in the USA (Sabine pass, Lousiana) and the Dutch TTF price for Natural Gas.

Conclusion

My methods were unable to find a corelation between the two sets of data.
as shown by my mean average error (MAE) being worse than the baseline control
and my root mean squared error (rmse) being bigger than my MAE.

It is most probably a much more complex relationship between the number
of exports and the TTF Price. Such Factors may include: Geopolitics,
European LNG storage capability and the ever changing demand
of natural gas
